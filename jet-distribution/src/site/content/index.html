JET (Java Econometrics Toolkit) aims to provide a high-quality open-source (under a commercially-friendly license) Java-based collection of econometrics tools.

JET modules

Regression

The jet-regression module includes:

  • Estimators of Multiple Linear Regression: OLS and GLS
  • Estimators and minimisers of Information Criterion: Akaike, Hannan-Quinn, Schwarz

Hypothesis Testing

The jet-hypothesis module includes:

  • Estimators of critical values and p-values for several hypothesis tests: ECM, LRC, URC, Johansen.
